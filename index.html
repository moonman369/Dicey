📝 INSTRUCTIONS


Welcome to Dicey!!! This is a 2 player dice game.


CONTROLS:


🎲 ROLL DICE -> A random dice roll that gives any number between 1 and 6 (both inclusive).
📥 HOLD -> Hold the current score and end your turn.
🔄 NEW GAME -> Reset the scores and start a new game.


HOW TO PLAY:


1. Every 'ROLL' increases your 'CURRENT SCORE' by the amount you have rolled but comes back to `0` if you roll a  `1` and your turn gets over.
2. Basically, you can keep rolling the dice till you roll a 1.
3. Note, that your current score does not add to your actual score unless you 'HOLD' it. But once you 'HOLD' a score your turn also gets over
4. The first player to reach a score of 100, wins the game.
